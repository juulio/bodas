How does the gift list work:

Step 1

Enter to the GIFT LIST section. There, different gift options will be displayed classified by country. Pamela and José Pablo will travel through Spain, France and Italy.

Step 2

Once you have chosen your gift option, you must click on the "GIFT" button. A window with a form will show up. Please fill out all the spaces (Name, Email address and Message) and then click on the "SEND" button.

Step 3

At this moment you will receive an email confirming the reservation of the gift you selected along with the banking information, so that you can make the corresponding deposit or wire transfer. Once the deposit or transfer has been made, the bride and groom will receive your gift certificate.

*It is only possible to make one gift at a time.
